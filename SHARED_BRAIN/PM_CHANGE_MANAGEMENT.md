# CHANGE MANAGEMENT

---

## ORIGINAL TEXT:

**In summary, PMs follow four steps to effectively manage changes and avoid disruptions to the project.**

- Step 1: Identify change – PMs assess the change request, clarifies its scope, and documents key details in a project change request form before making an initial decision to approve, reject, or defer it.
- Step 2: Investigate the change – PMs analyze the requested change to determine the change's impact on scope, schedule, and budget, while also considering alternative solutions.
- Step 3: Estimate costs – PMs evaluate the financial and resource implications of the change, ensuring even minor adjustments are properly reviewed.
- Step 4: Approve the change – Once stakeholders approve the change, PMs update the project plan, including schedule, budget, and resource allocation.
