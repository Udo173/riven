# BEST PRACTICES FOR EFFECTIVE REPORTING

---

## ORIGINAL TEXT:

**Here's what PMs do to write comprehensive and robust reports.**

- Maintain clarity and brevity: PMs avoid overwhelming stakeholders with excessive detail, and focus on key metrics, risks, and action items.
- Use visual aids: PMs use charts, graphs, and timelines to communicate complex information clearly and quickly.
- Tailor content to the audience: PMs customize the depth and complexity of the report depending on the audience; executives typically need summaries, while team members need detailed information.
- Ensure consistency: PMs adhere to regular reporting schedules so stakeholders know when to expect updates and can plan accordingly.
- Focus on action items: PMs prioritize completed tasks, current work, and decisions that need to be made to keep everyone aligned and moving forward.
