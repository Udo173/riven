## ORIGINAL (komplett):

**Creating a risk management plan helps PMs proactively address uncertainties and deliver projects timely, but managing risks efficiently requires continuous analysis and quick decision making.**

**In summary, PMs follow five steps to create the risk management plan and effectively manage risks throughout the project.**

- Step 1: Identify risks – PMs uncover and document potential risks that can affect the project.
- Step 2: Assess and prioritize risks – PMs prioritize the risks based on their potential impact.
- Step 3: Analyze risks quantitatively – PMs conduct a quantitative analysis to measure the impact of prioritized risks on cost, time, and scope.
- Step 4: Plan risk responses – PMs select appropriate responses and management strategies, such as avoidance, mitigation, transfer, or acceptance.
- Step 5: Monitor and adapt the approach – PMs continuously track status and update the approaches to manage the identified risks throughout the project lifecycle.
